The Vision

Born from the core.

CORE BORN is an art toy brand where every piece carries a story, a personality, a soul. Designed at the intersection of fine art, street culture, and craftsmanship.

Behind every piece is Serdar Khydyrov — the sole creator of CORE BORN. Each art toy is conceived, sculpted, and finished entirely by hand. No shortcuts, no mass production.
